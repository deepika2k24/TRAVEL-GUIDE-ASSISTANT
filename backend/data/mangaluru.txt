A bustling coastal city where beaches meet temples, spice markets, and seafood trails
Mangaluru, a vibrant port city nestled along Karnataka’s coastline, blends the energy of urban life with the serenity of the sea. Known for its ancient temples, colonial architecture, palm-fringed beaches, and rich culinary heritage, the city is a cultural crossroads where tradition meets modernity. Whether you’re strolling through bustling markets or relaxing by the Arabian Sea, Mangaluru offers a distinctive coastal charm that’s both rooted and refreshing.
